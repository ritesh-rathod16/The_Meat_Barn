Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery timings?

We deliver daily from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Orders placed before 6:00 PM will be delivered the next day. For specific time slots, you can contact our customer support after placing your order.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive an SMS and email with a tracking link. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 24 hours of delivery if the product is damaged or not as described. Please contact our customer support immediately if you need to return an item.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer special discounts for bulk orders. Please contact our sales team at bulk@farmfresh.com or call +91 98765 43210 for customized quotes.

How do I change or cancel my order?

You can change or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it by calling our customer support. After that, changes may not be possible as we begin processing your order immediately.
